About astral adhesives

Astral Adhesives manufactures diversified range of adhesives, sealants, putties and construction aids. Our strength is backward integration in almost all product categories which gives us advantage to maintain our quality standards and develop innovative products to meet requirement of our customer.

Why astral adhesives

Our products are mostly pioneering efforts where advantage has been taken of our knowledge of different chemistries such as epoxy, silicones, acrylics, cyanoacrylates, UV cures PVC etc and knowledge of customer needs to design products that meet.

We ensure good manufacturing practices, segregation of production facilities for every grade and the latest control systems that contribute to consistency of our products. Astral Adhesives products stand for quality and reliability and are marked through a network of about 2000+ distributors all over country.
