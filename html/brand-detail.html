Trubuild

Trubuild BWP (Binder & Water proofer) is an acrylic co-polymer emulsion which is used as bonding agent for new substrate to old substrates and as cement mortar modifier which gives a excellent bond to old and new concrete. If added in concrete, It increases the tensile and flexural strength and life of concrete. If it mix with cement to make a slurry then it gives waterproofing coating.
